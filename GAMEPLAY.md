# Gameplay

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| `←` / `→` | Move left / right |
| `Space` | Jump |
| `Z` | Attack |

## How to Play

Start the game from the title screen by pressing **Space** or **Z**.

Navigate the platforms and reach the boss to fight it. Attack the boss while
avoiding its strikes and the hazards on the ground.

## Win / Loss Conditions

> These are works in progress and will be updated as the game develops.

**You win** when the boss's health bar is fully depleted.

**You lose** when your own health bar reaches zero — this can happen by taking
hits from the boss or by touching a hazard on the ground.

After either outcome, press **Space** or **Z** to play again.

## Health Bars

| Bar | Location | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Green (player) | Top-left | Your remaining health — starts at 3 |
| Red (boss) | Top-right | Boss remaining health — starts at 5 |
